# HTF Wick Sweep Order Block Indicator

## Session Onboarding Procedure

---

At the beginning of every session:

### Step 1

Read `REPOSITORY_MANIFEST.md`

### Step 2

Read `AGENT_PROTOCOL.md`

### Step 3

Read `CURRENT_STATUS.md`

### Step 4

Read `SESSION_HANDOFF.md`

### Step 5

Read `docs/V9_SPEC.md`

### Step 6

Read `DECISIONS.md`

### Step 7

Read `LESSONS_LEARNED.md`

### Step 8

Audit `htf_wick_sweep_ob.pine`

### Step 9

Determine current milestone (from `CURRENT_STATUS.md` + `ROADMAP.md`)

### Step 10

Determine technical debt (from `KNOWN_ISSUES.md`)

### Step 11

Propose next implementation step

---

# Golden Rule

Do not write code until steps 1-11 complete.
